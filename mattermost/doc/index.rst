Mattermost
==========

.. from: http://www.mattermost.org/ 2015-12-27

Mattermost is an open source, on-prem Slack-alternative.
Mattermost is modern communication behind your firewall.
As an alternative to proprietary SaaS messaging, Mattermost brings all your
team communication into one place, making it searchable and accessible
anywhere.
