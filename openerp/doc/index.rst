OpenERP
=======

Introduction
------------

OpenERP now call Odoo is a suite of open-source business apps
written in :doc:`/python/doc/index` and released under the `AGPL license
<http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Affero_General_Public_License>`_. It is used by 2
million users worldwide to manage companies of all different sizes. The main
OpenERP components are the server, 260 core modules (also called official
modules) and around 4000 community modules.
